# Prompt/Response 009 - Why "Vacation Without Hotels"

## Prompt summary
Why is "vacation without hotels" the best framing? What friction do hotels create that a mobile home solves?

## Response highlights
- Hotels are not bad; they are optimized for fixed-location schedules.
- Mobile home is optimized for movement + spontaneity.
- Core hotel pain points solved:
  - Check-in/check-out workflow friction
  - Repacking fatigue
  - Commute from room to destination
  - Privacy inconsistency
  - Cost stacking for groups/long stays
- Strong paired messaging:
  - "חופשה בלי מלונות"
  - "המלון שלך על גלגלים"
